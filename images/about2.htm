2019 Kathryn Hall Cabernet Sauvignon

The Kathryn Hall Cabernet Sauvignon is the pinnacle of our blending efforts each year. The exalted Sacrashe vineyard takes center stage, and is the basis of this blend. From there, we search out complementary vineyards, from the most regarded mountain appellations, to create balance, symmetry and finesse in the wine.

Inky garnet in color. Open, effusive nose with freshly turned earth, dark spice, chocolate, ripe dark blackberry, and graphite. Supple on the entry; lush, broad, opulent, full of mid-palate fruit and sleek tannin with a lengthy finish.

2017 Jack's Masterpiece Cabernet Sauvignon

Our Jacks Masterpiece comes from many vineyard sources and appellations, but tends to be dominated by mountain fruit. Vineyards planted in mountain terrains produce vines smaller in stature but with more concentrated grapes. These grapes provide the basis for the dense, powerful structure of this wine.

In this vintage, aromas of dark spice, powerful black fruit, and spicy barrel character abound. The flavors are layered and dense - black cherry, dark brambly fruit, pepper, and clove are supported by ultra-dense tannin. This wine is about power and structure - mountain fruit and robust tannin.

2018 Eighteen Seventy-Three Cabernet Sauvignon

The fruit for our Eighteen Seventy-Three hails from the rugged mountain slopes that frame Napa Valley. Power and individuality underscore this wine, a deliberate intent to commemorate the trailblazing spirit of Napas first vintners.

Deep garnet color. Complex fruit aromas of ripe blackberry, subtle warm oak and plum. On the palate, pronounced flavors of dark fruit, cocoa nib, wet stone, and baking spice are supported by firm, structured tannins and bright acidity round out the powerful finish.
